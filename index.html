<table class="table-bio">
<td class="col-7">
<p style="text-align:justify; text-justify:inter-ideograph;padding-right:20px;">
Henghui Ding is currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at <a href="https://vision.ee.ethz.ch/" target="_blank">Computer Vision Lab</a> of <a href="https://ethz.ch/en.html" target="_blank">ETH Zürich</a> in Switzerland, working with Prof. <a href="https://www.yf.io/" target="_blank">Fisher Yu</a>. He was a Research Scientist at ByteDance AI Lab in Singapore. He received the Ph.D. degree (winner of Best Thesis Award) from Nanyang Technological University (<a href="https://www.ntu.edu.sg/Pages/home.aspx" target="_blank">NTU</a>), Singapore, advised by Prof. <a href="https://personal.ntu.edu.sg/exdjiang/" target="_blank">Xudong Jiang</a>, and Prof. <a href="https://sites.google.com/site/gangwang6/" target="_blank">Gang Wang</a>. Prior to that, he received the B.E. degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University (<a href="http://en.xjtu.edu.cn/" target="_blank">XJTU</a>), Xi'an, China. His research interests include computer vision, language, machine learning, with special emphasis on high-level image/video recognition and pixel-level scene understanding. <br>
